# Optimisation-S8
9 hours (3x3 hours) in practical classes in the 4th year at INSA Hauts-de-France in the specialization Mechanical and Energy Engineering.

- Formulation and analysis of an optimisation problem
- Unconstrained differential optimisation on R^n: optimality conditions, descent algorithms: fixed-step gradient, locally optimal step size, Newton’s method, BGFS,
Wolfe’s step
- Differential optimisation with equality and inequality constraints on R^n: Lagrange multipliers, KKT conditions, projected gradient algorithm, Lagrangian,
duality, Uzawa’s algorithm

Originally created in Jupyter and then converted for GitHub.
